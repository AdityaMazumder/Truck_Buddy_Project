What is TruckBuddy?

At TruckBuddy, we understand the importance of keeping your trucks in top condition to ensure safety, efficiency, and longevity. Our platform is designed to provide timely maintenance reminders, helping you stay on top of all your truck care needs. With TruckBuddy, you can easily schedule and track maintenance tasks, receive alerts for upcoming services, and access detailed maintenance logs. Whether you manage a single truck or an entire fleet, TruckBuddy simplifies the process, giving you peace of mind and more time to focus on your business. Join TruckBuddy today and experience hassle-free truck maintenance management!

Why TruckBuddy?

  • Reliable Maintenance Reminders:TruckBuddy ensures that you never miss a maintenance check. Our reliable reminder system alerts you ahead of time, helping you keep your trucks in optimal condition and avoiding unexpected breakdowns.
  • Comprehensive Service Tracking: Easily track all your maintenance tasks with TruckBuddy. From oil changes to tire rotations, our platform keeps a detailed log of every service, providing you with a complete maintenance history for each truck.
  • User-Friendly Interface: Our intuitive interface makes it simple to schedule and manage maintenance tasks. Whether you're a fleet manager or an owner-operator, TruckBuddy's user-friendly design ensures that you can quickly access and update your maintenance records.
  • Customizable Alerts: Tailor your maintenance alerts to suit your specific needs. TruckBuddy allows you to set reminders based on time, mileage, or engine hours, ensuring that your trucks receive timely care based on your usage patterns.
